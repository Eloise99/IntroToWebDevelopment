Harmony Restaurant is located in the beautiful city of Inverness in northern Scotland. Enjoy the hearty food and junkshop aesthetic either as a friendly and filling brunch or a classy and cosy evening meal. We are a family business established in 1883 and are proud of our heritage, many of our meals use recipes that have been handed down the generations - we hope you enjoy them as much as we have!
