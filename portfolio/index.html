About Me

I am Eloise and I'm a 21 year old Mathematics student. I am from Brighton, in the south of England and love to live by the sea. I am currently doing an internship with mthree/NatWestMarkets, and am looking forward to learning more about coding!

I am entering my fourth year at Durham University, having enjoyed my time there immensely. After university, I either want to take on a PhD, or go into tech - I have enjoyed programming and mathematical modelling throughout my degree and would love to be able to apply this to the real world.

At school, I always enjoyed Maths, and remember taking part in the National Final of the UKMT Team Maths Challenge. It was a great experience, and the feeling of competing with many of the best mathematicians of our age across the country made it extremely enjoyable! I think that was one of the highlights of secondary school and really sparked my love for problem solving.

Outside of Mathematics, I have taken on challenges with regard to programming by completing an online course in HTML, and doing an additional module in the Computer Science department of my university. These experiences have enriched my programming knowledge greatly, and I have enjoyed exploring this area in more depth.
